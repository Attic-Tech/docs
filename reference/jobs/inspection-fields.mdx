This reference explains the inspection report that a salesperson completes for an estimate: what it records, which fields are required, who can create or change it, and the rules that govern when it can be saved or edited.

The user-facing roles referenced below are **Admin**, **Ops Manager**, **Sales Admin**, **Sales Member**, **Client Coordinator**, **Crew Leader**, and **Crew Member**. For the full breakdown of what each role can do, see [/reference/platform/permissions-roles-matrix](/reference/platform/permissions-roles-matrix).

## What the inspection report is

The inspection report is a structured record the salesperson completes for an estimate. It captures what they observed during the in-person walkthrough so the install crew can work confidently, avoid mistakes, and deliver a consistent customer experience.

There is exactly **one inspection report per estimate**. The report is tied to a single estimate and the link is one-to-one — there is never more than one report for the same estimate.

The report is organized into themed sections:

- Scheduling & Job Setup
- Customer Profile
- Property Details
- Walkthrough Notes
- Roof Information
- HVAC Information
- Crew Notes

## When the inspection report is available

The inspection report only appears when your workspace has the inspection-report feature turned on.

- **If the feature is off**, the report step is skipped in the estimate wizard, and any attempt to open the report view sends the user back to the estimate breakdown.
- **If the feature is on**, the report is the final step of the estimate wizard, reached after the last work area. The button to advance into it from the items step reads **Proceed to Report**.

## Who can create or change a report

<Note>
  Inspection-report access follows your workspace's role permissions. See [/reference/platform/permissions-roles-matrix](/reference/platform/permissions-roles-matrix) for the authoritative breakdown, and [/reference/platform/org-branch-scoping](/reference/platform/org-branch-scoping) for how visibility is scoped to your organization.
</Note>

- **Create and edit:** limited to sales roles within the workspace — Admin, Sales Admin, and Sales Member.
- **View:** scoped to the user's own organization; most staff roles can read a report, and some roles are read-only.
- **Delete:** inspection reports are not deletable through the product; they are removed only by Menaia platform staff.

## What the report records

The lists below note which fields are **required** before the report can be left. A few fields are required by the form even though the saved record itself would accept them empty — those are called out as "required in the form."

### Scheduling & Job Setup

- Whether the customer is flexible on scheduling — **required**.
- An estimated number of days of work — **required**. This value lives on the estimate and is shown here. It is hidden when every selected item is a sub-contracted item.
- Whether scheduling depends on outside factors (for example, other vendors or client limitations) — optional free-text explanation.
- Whether the job needs special materials — optional free-text explanation.

### Customer Profile

- A description of the customer's demeanor (for example, calm, detail-oriented, emotional) — **required**. Submitting it empty is rejected with "Customer demeanor is required."
- What matters most to the customer (for example, cleanliness, air seal, rodents) — **required in the form** ("Customer concerns is required"). The saved record treats this as optional, so the requirement is enforced by the form rather than the stored data.

### Property Details

This section mixes property-level questions (stored on the property) with one report field.

- Number of stories — **required**.
- Whether the property is occupied — **required**.
- Estimated parking distance from the front door — **required**.
- Whether there are pet concerns the crew must know about — **required**.
- Known obstacles or hazards (for example, low ceilings, tight access, fragile items, pets) — **required** free text ("Hazards is required").
- Special instructions for box-truck parking or property access (for example, gate code, lockbox) — **required** free text ("Special instructions is required").
- Unique structural features that could impact the installation — **required in the form** ("Installation impacts is required"); optional on the saved record.

### Walkthrough Notes

- Any visible damage seen during the walkthrough — **required** multi-select. The choices are: no pre-existing damage, ceiling drywall cracks, wall cracks or chips, baseboard chips, floor scratching, light-fixture damage, driveway stains, broken attic lid, broken attic ladder, front-yard damage, and other.
- A free-text description of any damage selected above — optional.

### Roof Information

All six roof questions are **required**:

- **Roof material** — asphalt shingles, clay tiles, cement tiles, woodshaker, metal, torch down, rubber, or other.
- **Roof age** (consult with client) — under 5 years, 5–10, 11–20, over 20, or client unsure.
- **Roof condition** — good, fair, or needs replacement.
- **Roof decking type** — plywood, slates, or none.
- **Whether the roof is walkable** — yes or no.
- **Whether the customer wants a full roof inspection** — yes or no.

Roofing photos and videos can be attached in this section. The upload controls are hidden when the report is read-only.

### HVAC Information

- **Customer comfort level** — **required**; high, moderate, or low comfort.
- **HVAC age** — optional; same age choices as roof age.
- **HVAC system condition** — optional; good, fair, or needs replacement.
- **Air-ducts condition** — optional; good, fair, or needs replacement.
- **Whether the customer wants a full HVAC or furnace inspection** — **required**; yes or no.

HVAC photos and videos can be attached in this section. The upload controls are hidden when the report is read-only.

### Crew Notes

- Free-text notes to help the crew deliver a five-star job — optional, but capped at a maximum length. Exceeding the cap is rejected with "Crew notes must be N characters or fewer."
- Crew notes have an optional AI assist:
  - **Enhance with AI** appears when notes already exist.
  - **Suggest with AI** appears when notes are empty but other context is present (demeanor, concerns, hazards, special instructions).
  - The AI control is unavailable when there is neither notes nor context, and it is hidden entirely in read-only view.
- If AI enhancement fails, an "Enhancement failed" message appears and the notes are left as they were. An applied AI suggestion can be undone.

## Completion and submission rules

<Card title="Required-field gate" icon="list-check">
  To advance past the report, the required fields must be filled. If validation fails, the named error messages above appear and navigation is blocked. Required-but-empty selections and free-text fields each surface their own specific message.
</Card>

<Card title="The report saves with the estimate" icon="floppy-disk">
  The report is not submitted on its own — it is saved as part of saving the estimate, and only when the inspection-report feature is enabled. If the report save fails, the whole save fails with "Failed to save inspection report."
</Card>

<Card title="One report per estimate" icon="link">
  Saving creates the report the first time and updates the same one thereafter. Because it is keyed to the estimate, there is never more than one.
</Card>

<Card title="Locked once the estimate is Sold" icon="lock">
  An existing report cannot be updated once its estimate is in the **Sold** state. The save is rejected with "Cannot update a report with a Sold estimate."
</Card>

<Card title="The estimate must exist" icon="circle-exclamation">
  Saving a report for an estimate that cannot be found (or that is not in the user's organization) is rejected with "Job estimate not found."
</Card>

## Read-only viewing

- An existing estimate that is **not In Progress**, or the dedicated report-viewer mode, opens the report **read-only**: a print-style summary (job name, salesperson, date, client info, work details) with a Print button, every field disabled, and the photo-upload controls hidden.
- In read-only mode, the wizard deliberately skips required-field validation when paging through, so an older report saved before a field became required can still be viewed.
- To make changes to a read-only estimate's report, set the estimate back to **In Progress** from the estimates list. Attempting to edit while read-only shows "This estimate is read-only. Change its status to In Progress from the estimates list to make changes."

## Related AI estimate review

When the AI-calculator feature is enabled, the inspection report's contents are fed into an automated estimate-quality review that can surface info, warning, or error notes and item suggestions. It returns no warnings when the estimate looks fine. This review is **advisory only** and does not block submission.

## Quick reference

- **One inspection report per estimate**, captured by the salesperson during the walkthrough.
- **Create or edit:** Admin, Sales Admin, Sales Member. Reports are not deletable in the product.
- **Available only when the inspection-report feature is on**; it is the final step of the estimate wizard.
- **Saved with the estimate**, never on its own; required fields are enforced by the form before the report can be left.
- **Locked once the estimate is Sold**; opens **read-only** for any estimate that is not In Progress.
- Roof and HVAC sections accept **photos and videos**, hidden in read-only view.
