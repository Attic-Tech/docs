A **Win Post** is a celebration you share in your team feed when something good happens — a new sale closes, your crew saves hours on a job, or someone unlocks a badge. Instead of writing it from scratch, you pick the win and let AI draft a friendly, ready-to-share post. You stay in control: review the wording, tweak it, add a GIF, and post when it looks right.

<Note>
You don't have to write anything. AI turns the win into a post for you — your job is to pick the right win and make sure it reads the way you want before you share it.
</Note>

## Start a win post

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your team feed">
    Go to your team **feed** from the main navigation. The box at the top — where it says "Share a win, shout out a teammate" — is where new posts begin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Use AI">
    In the row of buttons under the post box, click **Use AI** (look for the sparkle icon). On a phone, tap the **+** button and choose **Use AI** from the menu. This opens the **Create Win Post** window.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The post box also has **Gif** and **Media** buttons if you'd rather write a post by hand. **Use AI** is the fastest way to celebrate a specific win that the system already knows about.
</Tip>

## Walk through the window

The Create Win Post window moves through three steps. The buttons at the bottom — **Back** and **Continue** (or **Review Post** and **Share Post** on later steps) — carry you forward and back.

### Step 1 — Pick a win to celebrate

The first screen, **Create Win Post**, shows a list of recent wins that haven't been posted yet. Each win appears as a card with:

- The **win type** — **New Sale**, **Job Hours Saved**, or **Badge Unlocked**.
- The **customer or job** it relates to, and the headline number (the sale amount or the hours saved) in green.
- The **teammates** involved, shown with their names and photos.
- The **date** the win happened.

To narrow the list, use the **All Wins** dropdown at the top to show only one type — for example, just **New Sale** wins.

Click the win you want to celebrate so it's highlighted, then click **Continue**.

<Note>
If you see "No unposted wins yet," it just means there's nothing new to celebrate right now. When your team logs a sale, saves hours on a job, or unlocks a badge, it'll show up here ready to post.
</Note>

### Step 2 — Add your details

This screen shows the win you picked at the top, then lets you add a personal touch before the AI writes anything.

- **Add a note (optional).** Type anything you'd like the post to mention — a shout-out to a teammate, a detail about the job, or the reason this win mattered. The AI works your note into the post. There's room for a short paragraph.
- **Add Gif.** Click **Add Gif** to search for and pick a GIF to attach. Once added, you can swap it or use **Delete Gif** to remove it.

When you're ready, click **Review Post**. The AI gets to work writing your post.

<Tip>
A note is optional, but it's the easiest way to make the post feel personal. Mention who deserves credit or what made the win special, and the AI will fold it in.
</Tip>

### Step 3 — Review and post

This is the **Review & Post** screen. You'll briefly see "Generating post..." while the AI writes, then your draft appears under **Generated Post**.

Here you can:

- **Edit the wording.** The draft is fully editable — click into it and change anything you like. Teammate names appear as tags so everyone involved gets credited.
- **Regenerate Post.** Not happy with the draft? Click **Regenerate Post** for a fresh version. Your note and GIF are kept.
- **Add or change the GIF.** If you attached a GIF, you can swap or remove it right here.

When the post reads the way you want, click **Share Post**. Your win goes live in the team feed and the window closes.

<Note>
The AI gives you a head start, not the final word. Always read the draft before sharing — it's your post, and you can change any of it.
</Note>

## After you post

Once you click **Share Post**, you'll see a "Post shared" confirmation and your win appears in the feed for the whole team to see, react to, and comment on. The win drops off the "pick a win" list so it won't get posted twice.

<Tip>
Changed your mind mid-way? Close the window at any time and nothing is posted. Your note, GIF, and selection only get shared when you click **Share Post**.
</Tip>
