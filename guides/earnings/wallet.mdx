Your **Wallet** is where you keep an eye on the bonus you're earning. It rolls up every job that has added to (or subtracted from) your bonus into one running total, and it lists each job so you can see exactly where the number comes from.

<Note>
The figures in your Wallet are a **projection** of your current standing, not a final paycheck. They can still move as job-related costs are added, so treat them as a live estimate of where you stand rather than a settled amount.
</Note>

## Open your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to My Wallet">
    Open **My Wallet** from the navigation. The page loads with your numbers already filled in — there's nothing to set up first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the top three cards">
    The three cards across the top give you the headline view of your bonus. Everything below them is the job-by-job detail.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The Wallet isn't switched on for every account. If you don't see **My Wallet** in your navigation, it isn't enabled for you yet — your manager can tell you whether it's available.
</Note>

## Understand the three cards

The top of the page has three summary cards. Each one is also a quick filter — clicking a card narrows the list below it to match.

- **Total Earnings** — your overall projected bonus, shown in green when it's positive and red when it's negative. The note underneath (for example, *From 24 jobs*) tells you how many jobs went into the total.
- **Positive Payouts** — how many jobs **added** bonus. These are the wins that paid out.
- **Bonus Deductions** — how many jobs **subtracted** bonus, labelled *Penalties incurred*. These are the jobs where a deduction was applied.

<Tip>
Click **Positive Payouts** to see only the jobs that earned bonus, or **Bonus Deductions** to see only the ones that cost you. Click **Total Earnings** to go back to the full list.
</Tip>

## Read the transactions list

Below the cards is the full list of jobs that make up your bonus. Each row shows:

- **Job Name** — the job the bonus is tied to.
- **Date** — when the job completed. If it hasn't completed yet, you'll see **TBD**.
- **Transaction Type** — the amount this job added or subtracted, in green with a **+** when it added bonus, and in red when it took bonus away.

If nothing matches your current filters, the list shows **No transactions match the selected filters** — clear or change a filter to see your jobs again.

## Filter and search

Use the controls just above the list to zero in on the jobs you care about.

- **Search by job name** — type any part of a job's name to find it.
- **Filter by date** — choose **All Time**, **Last 7 Days**, **Last 30 Days**, or **Last 90 Days**.
- **Filter by type** — choose **All Transactions**, **Positive Only**, or **Negative Only**. (This is the same control as clicking the cards up top.)

<Tip>
Filters stack. Pick **Last 30 Days** and **Positive Only** together to see just the bonus you earned in the past month.
</Tip>

When there's more than one page of results, use **Previous** and **Next page** at the bottom right to move through them. The page counter (for example, *Page 1 of 3*) tells you where you are.

## If you manage a team

Managers get one extra control and one extra column.

- A **Filter by user** picker appears next to the search box. Pick a person on your branch to view their projected bonus and their job list instead of your own.
- The list gains a **User Name** column so you can see who each job belongs to while you're browsing across the team.

<Note>
Leave the user picker empty to see your own Wallet. Choose someone to switch the whole page — cards, totals, and list — to that person's standing.
</Note>
