**Integrations** is where you connect Menaia to add-on apps that extend what your workspace can do — for example, accepting online payments or linking your accounting. Each integration has its own page that explains what it does, what it costs, and how to turn it on.

<Note>
Integrations are an admin area. You'll need an **Admin** role to open the Integrations page and connect an app. If you don't see **Integrations** in the main navigation, you don't currently have access.
</Note>

## Open the marketplace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    Choose **Integrations** from the main navigation. This opens the marketplace — a grid of every app that's available to your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the app you want">
    Use the **Search apps** box to find an integration by name or description, or use the **All Categories** dropdown to narrow the list to a single category (such as **Payments** or **Accounting**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open an app's page">
    Click any app card to open its detail page, where you can read the full description and connect it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If the marketplace looks empty or your search comes up short, you'll see a short message in the middle of the page. "No integrations available" means there's nothing to connect for your workspace yet; "No results found" means your search or category filter is hiding everything — clear the search box or switch back to **All Categories** to see the full list again.
</Tip>

## Read an app's detail page

Every integration's page is laid out the same way, so you always know where to look.

- **About this app** describes what the integration does and the key benefits — both for your team and for your customers.
- The **Get Started** panel on the right is where you connect the app. It includes a short summary and a connect button.
- The quick facts under the panel tell you the **Pricing**, the typical **Setup Time**, and the **Support** availability at a glance.

To return to the full list at any time, click **Back to Marketplace** at the top of the page.

## Connect Menaia Pay

**Menaia Pay** is the built-in way to accept online payments and manage transactions directly inside Menaia. When it's available to your workspace, you'll find it in the **Payments** category of the marketplace.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Menaia Pay page">
    From the marketplace, click the **Menaia Pay** card to open its detail page and review what it offers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the connection">
    In the **Get Started** panel on the right, click **Connect Stripe** to begin. You'll be guided through creating a Stripe account — or linking the one you already have — so Menaia can process payments on your behalf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish setup">
    Complete the short setup — most workspaces are up and running in a few minutes. Once connected, payments, fees, and payouts stay in sync with your records automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Connecting a payment app is a one-time action handled by an admin — individual users don't need to set anything up themselves. Online payment collection is enabled per branch, so if a branch can't collect payments after connecting, reach out to Menaia to have online payments enabled for it.
</Note>

### What Menaia Pay unlocks

Once connected, your team can accept payments anywhere, collect deposits online, add service fees, and let customers pay by card or bank transfer — including paying several invoices at once through a direct link. Payments flow straight into your invoicing, so there's far less manual reconciliation to do.

<Tip>
Some integrations only appear for workspaces where the feature has been enabled. If you expected to see an app and it isn't listed, it may not be turned on for your workspace yet.
</Tip>

## QuickBooks

**QuickBooks** is the marketplace's accounting integration — you'll find it under the **Accounting** category. Open its card to read what it offers and connect it from its **Get Started** panel, the same way as any other app.

## Data Sources

**Data Sources** connects the tools your business already runs on — QuickBooks, Stripe, Salesforce, Google Sheets and hundreds more — so Meni can answer questions that span all of your business data. You'll find it under the **Data** category.

Unlike a single-app integration, its detail page is a hub with two parts: **Connected sources**, which lists everything you've already linked, and **Add a source**, a searchable catalog of connectors you can add to.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Data Sources">
    From the marketplace, open the **Data Sources** card. If you haven't linked anything yet, click **Browse connectors** to jump to the catalog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a connector">
    Search the **Add a source** catalog by name, then click **Connect** on the source you want. Menaia opens a secure window where you authorize the connection — Menaia never stores your source's credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the first sync">
    After you authorize, the source appears under **Connected sources** and starts syncing in the background. It shows a **Syncing** badge during its first sync, then **Connected** along with when it was last synced.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each connected source shows its current status and lets you manage it:

- **Connected** — live and syncing on a schedule. **Syncing** means its first sync is still running.
- **Incomplete** — authorization didn't finish; use **Finish setup** to complete it.
- **Needs attention** — the connection broke; use **Reconnect** to fix it.
- Use the refresh control to re-check a source's status, or remove a source with the trash icon. Removing it stops syncing and Meni can no longer answer questions from it — you can reconnect it later.

<Tip>
Once your sources have finished their first sync, use **Ask Meni** to ask questions that span everything you've connected — for example, comparing figures across your accounting, payments, CRM, and spreadsheets in one place.
</Tip>
