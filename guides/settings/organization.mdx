The **Organization** page is where you set the core details for your company — its name, time zone, logo, and payroll ID. These settings shape how your company appears across the app and on the documents you send to customers, so it's worth getting them right.

<Note>
The Organization page is an admin setting. If you don't see **Settings** in the navigation, or the **Organization** item isn't listed, your role doesn't include managing company settings. Ask an administrator to make changes for you.
</Note>

## Open the Organization page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Open **Settings** from the navigation. You'll land on the settings area with a sidebar of categories on the left.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Organization">
    Under the **General** group, select **Organization**. This opens the page where you manage your company's basic information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Company Details

The **Company Details** section holds the two settings that identify your company throughout the app.

- **Company Name** — Your company's name as you want it to appear. This is the name shown across the app and on customer-facing documents, so enter it exactly as you'd like customers to see it. It's required.
- **Timezone** — The time zone your company operates in. This keeps dates, times, and scheduling consistent for everyone on your team. Choose from Los Angeles (Pacific), Denver (Mountain), Chicago (Central), or New York (Eastern). It's required.

<Tip>
Setting the right time zone up front keeps your calendar, scheduling, and timestamps accurate. If your team works across regions, pick the zone your main office runs on.
</Tip>

## Payroll

The **Payroll** section has a single optional field.

- **Organization Payroll ID** — An identifier that links your company to your external payroll system. Leave it blank if you don't connect to an outside payroll system. If you do, enter the identifier your payroll provider uses for your company.

## Organization Logo

The **Organization Logo** is your company's image. Once set, it can appear on customer-facing documents and across the app to give everything a consistent, branded look.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a logo">
    In the **Organization Logo** panel, drag an image onto the upload area or click it to choose a file from your device. You can upload a PNG, JPEG, or WebP image, up to 20MB.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Replace a logo">
    When a logo is already set, choose **Upload New** to swap in a different image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove a logo">
    To clear the current logo, choose **Delete Image**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
For the sharpest result, use a square image at the recommended size of 256 by 256 pixels. The logo uploads on its own — you don't need to save the page for a logo change to take effect.
</Tip>

## Save your changes

When you edit the **Company Name**, **Timezone**, or **Organization Payroll ID**, click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the page to apply them.

<Note>
The **Save Changes** button stays inactive until you've actually changed something. Once you make an edit, it turns on. If a required field is empty — like the company name — you'll see a prompt to fill it in before the page will save.
</Note>

Logo changes are the exception: uploading or deleting a logo takes effect immediately and doesn't depend on **Save Changes**.
