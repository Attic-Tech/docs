<Note>
Generating an estimate from a written or spoken description is no longer available in Menaia.
</Note>

You can still build an estimate step by step with the [Calculator](/guides/estimates/create), and when AI is turned on for your branch you can [enhance crew notes with AI](/guides/estimates/enhance-crew-notes).
