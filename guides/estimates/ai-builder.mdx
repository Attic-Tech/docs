When the AI Calculator is turned on for your branch, a brand-new estimate can build itself from a short description of the job. You tell it what the work is, and it fills in the work areas and items so you're starting from a real draft instead of a blank page.

<Note>
AI gives you a head start, not the final word. Always review the breakdown it produces before you save or send anything.
</Note>

## Before you start

On the first step of the Calculator, choose the **Client Name** and the **project** this estimate belongs to. The AI builder needs both before it can generate anything — until you pick them, you'll see a reminder to **Select a client and project above before generating an estimate**.

## Describe the job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Describe tab">
    On the AI builder, make sure the **Describe** tab is selected (it's next to the **Photos** tab).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type what the job is">
    In the **Describe the job scope…** box, write a plain-language description of the work — for example, *"2500 sqft attic, blow in R-38 cellulose"* or *"Crawl space vapor barrier and insulation"*. Press **Enter** or the send button when you're ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer any follow-up questions">
    If the AI needs a detail to get it right, it shows a short question with a few choices. Tap the option that fits, and it keeps building.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the result">
    The AI shows an **Estimate Generated** card listing the items it added to each work area, plus a suggested number of days. Read it over to make sure it matches the job.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue to the breakdown">
    Choose **Accept & Review Breakdown** to jump to the full summary, where you can fine-tune anything before saving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Not sure where to start? Tap one of the suggestion pills under the chat (like *"Remove old batts and re-insulate"*) to drop a ready-made description into the conversation.
</Tip>

## Speak it instead of typing

If your device supports it, you'll see a **microphone** button next to the description box.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start voice input">
    Tap the **microphone** to start. The box highlights and the button turns red while it's listening.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the job out loud">
    Speak naturally — your words appear in the box as you talk. You can keep talking to add more detail.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stop and send">
    Tap the button again to stop listening, tidy up the text if needed, then send it just like a typed description.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Keep refining with the chat assistant

After the first draft is in, a small **sparkle button** sits in the bottom corner of the screen. Open it to ask for changes in plain language — *"add a vapor barrier,"* *"what should I add?"* — and the assistant updates the estimate for you, showing an **Estimate Updated** card of exactly what it added, removed, or changed.

## Prefer to build it yourself?

You can skip AI at any time. Choose **Start manually** to build the estimate by hand using the Calculator wizard instead.
