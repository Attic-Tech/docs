The **Estimates** area is where you price jobs and follow them all the way to a "yes." You build each estimate in the **Calculator**, then track and update every estimate from one list.

<Note>
You don't need to know any pricing math. The Calculator handles the numbers — your job is to describe the work accurately.
</Note>
