The **Estimates** page is your home base. Every estimate you've started or finished lives here, and you can search, filter, sort, and reopen any of them. Open it from **Estimates** in the main navigation.

## Switch views: Table, Board, Insights

At the top of the page, view tabs let you look at your estimates three ways:

- **Table** — the default list view, described in the rest of this guide.
- **Board** — a card-per-estimate board with a column for each status. Each card shows the estimate's name, price, and the avatar of the person it belongs to. Search, filter, and sort from the top of the board just as you can on the Table. Drag a card into another column to change that estimate's status right from the board (the usual status rules still apply — for example, moving a signed estimate back to In Progress asks you to confirm removing the signature), or click a card to open that estimate in the Calculator.
- **Insights** — a dashboard view with key numbers across your estimates and an AI-written summary of what stands out.

## Read the list

Each row is one estimate. Depending on your branch's setup and your role, you'll see columns like:

- **Job Name**, **Client**, and **Project**
- **Created By** and **Updated At**
- **Address**
- **Status** (with a color-coded badge)
- **Client Viewed** and **Sent Date** (for proposals)
- **Sold Date** (for sold estimates)
- **Proposed Cost**
- **Work Areas**
- **Client Response** — when a customer has replied to a proposal, **View Response** opens their note

On a phone, each estimate shows as a tidy card with the same details and a menu of actions.

## Jump between status tabs

Above the list, tabs let you narrow to one status at a glance, each with a live count:

- **All**
- **In Progress**
- **Sold**
- **Released**
- **Lost**
- **Secondary**

Tap a tab to show only those estimates. Tap **All** to clear it.

## Search

Use the search box at the top — **Search by name or address…** — to jump straight to an estimate by its job name or property address.

## Filter

Open the filters to narrow the list further. You can filter by:

- **Sales Member** — whose estimates to show (available if you can see the whole branch's estimates)
- **Work Areas** — only estimates that include certain areas
- **Status** — pick one or more statuses
- **Date Created**, **Sold Date**, and **Sent Date** — choose a date range for each

Filters stack, so you can combine them — for example, *Sold* estimates with a *Sold Date* in the last month.

## Sort

Click a sortable column heading to sort by it, and click again to flip between ascending and descending. Your sort choice and column layout are remembered for next time.

## Open an estimate

From any row (or the **⋮** menu on mobile):

- **Edit** an estimate that's still **In Progress** to keep working on it in the Calculator. Estimates in other statuses open in **view** mode instead.
- Open the **proposal** (where proposals are enabled).
- Open the project's **media**.
- **Delete** an estimate (if your role allows it). Signed estimates can't be deleted.

<Tip>
If the list is empty after filtering, you'll see **No estimates found** with a shortcut to **Create a new estimate**.
</Tip>
