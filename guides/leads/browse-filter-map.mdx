The **Leads** page is your home base for working your pipeline. Every lead lives here, and you can search, filter, and even see them on a map — so you can quickly zero in on the leads that matter right now.

## Pick a view

At the top of the page, a **Table / Board / Insights** switch changes how you look at your pipeline:

- **Table** — the full list of leads, with search, filters, and a map option. This is the view the rest of this guide covers.
- **Board** — your leads as cards, arranged in columns by status, so you can see the whole pipeline stage by stage.
- **Insights** — a dashboard of pipeline metrics topped with an AI-generated summary of what's happening.

## Search your leads

At the top of the list is a search box: Search by name, email or phone.... Type any part of a contact's name, email, or phone number and the list narrows as you go. It's the fastest way to jump to a specific lead.

## Filter the pipeline

Click the Filters button to open the filter panel. Filters stack — turn on as many as you like to drill down. You can filter by:

- **Date Created** — pick a date range to see leads added in a window.
- **Branch** — show leads for a specific branch, or just the **Unassigned** ones.
- **Client Coordinators** — see leads handled by a particular coordinator.
- **Status** — check one or more pipeline statuses.
- **Tags** — filter by the labels you've applied to leads.
- **Confidence Level** — **Low**, **Medium**, or **High**.
- **Lead Source** — one or more sources the leads came from.
- **Lead Channel** — how the lead arrived: **Web Form**, **Phone Call**, or **Manual Entry**.
- **Created By** — who added the lead.

<Tip>
  The **Filters** button shows a count of how many filters are active, so you always know when your list is narrowed. Clear them to see everything again.
</Tip>

## Read the list

Each lead in the table shows the essentials at a glance — name, email, phone, lead source, channel, branch, confidence, status, client coordinator, address, tags, and when it was created. You can change a lead's **status** right from the list using the status dropdown on its row, without opening it.

Click any row to open the full **lead summary**.

<Note>
  On a phone, the list becomes easy-to-tap cards with the same key details, so you can work leads on the go.
</Note>

## Switch to the map view

Want to see _where_ your leads are? In the **Table** view, use the **List / Map** toggle near the top of the list to switch from the list to the map.

On the map:

- Each lead with an address shows up as a **pin**.
- Click a pin to see a quick popup with the **contact name**, **phone**, **email**, and **branch**.
- Click **View Lead Summary** in the popup to open the full lead.
- Pan and zoom — the map loads the leads in the area you're looking at.

<Tip>
  Your search and filters apply to the map too. Filter down to, say, high-confidence leads in one branch, then switch to the map to plan a day of visits by neighborhood.
</Tip>
