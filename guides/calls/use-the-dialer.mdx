Once your workspace is [connected to Aircall](/guides/settings/aircall), a phone lives right in Menaia's top bar. Your team can place calls, answer incoming ones, and see caller details — all without leaving the page they're working on.

<Note>
The phone panel appears when Calls is enabled for your workspace and Aircall is connected. It's available on **desktop** screens, and each person signs in with their **own Aircall account** the first time they open it — you need an Aircall seat to use the dialer.
</Note>

## Open the phone

Click the **phone icon** in the top bar. The **Phone** panel opens with Aircall's dialer inside — sign in once and you're set. The icon also keeps you informed at a glance:

- A **status dot** shows your line's availability, and switches to the call state when you're on a call.
- During a call, a **timer** next to the icon shows how long you've been talking.

You can close the panel at any time — **calls keep ringing even while the panel is closed**, and your call isn't interrupted while you navigate around Menaia.

## When a call comes in

Two things happen the moment your line rings:

1. **In Menaia**, an **Incoming call** banner appears with the caller's name and direct links to their client, lead, or project — so you can open their records before you pick up. If the number doesn't match anyone, it shows **Unknown caller**.
2. **In Aircall**, the agent taking the call sees a caller card with the same information — the matched client, their latest projects, and latest leads — including on mobile or wherever else they answer Aircall calls.

The banner stays for the duration of the call and clears when it ends. Every call is logged automatically on the [Calls page](/guides/calls/browse-and-review-calls), whether it was answered in Menaia, in the Aircall app, or missed entirely.

## Placing a call

Dial from the **Phone** panel the same way you would in Aircall — type the number and call. The call is logged in Menaia like any other, matched to the client or lead the number belongs to.

## Troubleshooting

- **"The phone couldn't connect"** — the dialer needs **third-party cookies** for aircall.io. Allow them in your browser settings (or add an exception for aircall.io) and reload.
- **The panel is empty or won't load** — reload the page; if it persists, check that you can sign in at aircall.io with your seat.
- **No phone icon at all** — either Calls isn't enabled for your workspace yet, Aircall isn't [connected](/guides/settings/aircall), or you're on a small screen (the dialer is desktop-only).
